Get it on your TV
Screen Mirror (Android)
Any Android TV · No extra hardware
  1. Pull down notification shade → tap Cast / Smart View / Screen Cast
  2. Select your Android TV from the list
  3. Your full screen appears on the TV — open this app and pick a channel
Not visible? Settings → Connected devices → Cast
AirPlay (Apple TV)
iPhone/iPad → Apple TV · Same WiFi
  1. Swipe down from top-right → open Control Center
  2. Tap Screen Mirroring → select your Apple TV
  3. Full phone screen mirrors to TV including this app
Tap the AirPlay icon inside the video player itself for a cleaner direct stream to TV
Chromecast / Cast via Chrome
Android TV has Chromecast built in · Same WiFi
  1. Open this page in Chrome on your phone
  2. Chrome menu ⋮ → Cast
  3. Select your Android TV — it has Chromecast built in, no dongle needed
  4. Chrome casts the full tab including the video to your TV
Both devices must be on the same WiFi network
